About EduPay Pico
Smart Coaching Payment Manager
📦 Version

EduPay Pico v1.0.0 — a fully offline-first, single-file payment manager for coaching centers.

🎯 Purpose

Helps students and guardians track subject-wise coaching fees, monthly dues, and generate professional receipts — all stored locally on your device.

✨ Features
Subject ManagerAuto Due Tracking ReceiptsPDF Export BackupsLive Search
📖 How To Use

1. Add a subject with schedule & fee. 2. Visit Payment to settle due months. 3. Generate a receipt anytime from the Receipt page.
